About me

I was born in Arlington, Texas but raised in Round Rock, and I love the Austin area. I previously worked at Blizzard Entertainment as a Technical Support Specialist for several years. After about four years I decided I needed a change and enlisted in the United States Air Force where I did Jet Engine work on the B-1 Lancer. When my time in the Air Force was up, I decided that I should pursue coding, and any oppurtunities that it might present me

I am a Llama enthusiast and enjoy all things Llama, in truth it started as something of a joke in middle school that stuck. My friends and family all get me llama related presents anytime they come across them.

Some of my hobbies include, playing Video Games, such as World of Warcraft, League of Legends, Destiny, Player Unknown's Battleground, Counter Strike, and Warframe. I also enjoy hiking, fishing, going to live music shows, and spending time with my friends and family.

I have always had a passion for technology, and I started making my own basic websites for video games all the way back in middle school. I truly hope to transition this passion into a career.
